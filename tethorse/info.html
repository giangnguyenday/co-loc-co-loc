About Tet Lucky Horse

Tet Lucky Horse is a small digital gift that blends motion, 3D, and Vietnamese New Year blessings. Shake your phone to draw one of six lucky horses, each with a unique message and token.

How it works

  • Tap “Enable Motion” on iOS to allow shake detection.
  • Shake your phone (or press to draw) to reveal your lucky horse.
  • Your result opens on a separate reveal page.

Privacy

This experience runs fully on your device. No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted.

Credits
